# AXIOM Brand Guidelines

Version 1.0  
Autonomous BI Engine Brand System

---

# 1. Brand Overview

## Brand Name
AXIOM

## Full Product Name
AXIOM Autonomous BI Engine

## Brand Positioning
AXIOM is an enterprise-grade autonomous business intelligence platform that transforms raw datasets into actionable insights, professional reports, dashboards, and executive presentations through agentic AI workflows.

The platform combines:
- Autonomous reasoning
- Data analysis
- Multi-agent orchestration
- Business intelligence automation
- Explainable AI reporting

---

# 2. Brand Personality

AXIOM should feel:

| Trait | Description |
|---|---|
| Intelligent | Advanced AI reasoning and analysis |
| Professional | Enterprise-ready and trustworthy |
| Futuristic | Modern and innovation-driven |
| Precise | Accurate, data-driven, and reliable |
| Analytical | Built for insight and decision-making |
| Scalable | Suitable for startups and enterprises |

---

# 3. Brand Voice

## Tone
- Professional
- Confident
- Intelligent
- Clear
- Technical but understandable

## Writing Style
- Use concise and informative language
- Prioritize clarity over marketing buzzwords
- Emphasize automation, intelligence, and trust
- Focus on insights and decision-making

## Example Messaging

### Good
> AXIOM transforms complex datasets into decision-ready intelligence.

### Good
> Autonomous analysis powered by explainable AI workflows.

### Avoid
> Revolutionary AI magic for everyone.

### Avoid
> The coolest analytics platform ever built.

# 15. Do’s and Don’ts

## Do
- Use dark enterprise themes
- Use gradients consistently
- Keep layouts clean
- Prioritize readability
- Maintain professional tone

## Don’t
- Overcrowd dashboards
- Use random bright colors
- Add excessive animations
- Use playful typography
- Break visual consistency
